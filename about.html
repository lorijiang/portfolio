Hi, I'm Lori!👋

🇨🇳🇨🇦 I'm a second-generation Chinese-Canadian born and raised in Vancouver. In my everyday life I am empathetic, curious, creative and fascinated by different cultures and experiences. These interests have nurtered me into an open-minded designer.

💻👩‍🎓 As a current student at SFU SIAT (2022-present), I'm dedicated to crafting engaging user experiences through research-driven, user-centered designs.

🧠🎓 My journey began at UBC, where my lifelong curiosity about human behavior led me to graduate with a BA in psychology in 2021. However, I soon realized my passion lies in blending creativity with understanding, leading me to discover UX design during a transformative gap year. Now, I enthusiastically merge my background in psychology with my creative instincts to shape meaningful user interactions.

When I'm not studying, I like to paint 🎨, watch shows & movies 🎬, solo travel ✈, and discover new restaurants 🍜 and cafes 🍵 in Vancouver with friends!
